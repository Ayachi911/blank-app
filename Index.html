Research Experience

River Water Quality Classification — ML Internship

C-DAC Mohali · Government Research Institution

Problem: Classify Indian river water potability at national scale into actionable safety tiers from real-world physicochemical monitoring data. Dataset: 1,620 river monitoring stations across India, 19 parameters.

Method: Rule-based classification system encoding WHO and EPA thresholds. Random Forest for validation and feature-importance. Feature importance: Fecal Coliform (0.389), Total Coliform (0.250), BOD (0.197).

Result: Deployed as an interactive prediction interface. 961 of 1,620 monitored Indian river stations classified as Bad quality under WHO/EPA standards.

Python · Scikit-learn · Random Forest · Pandas · Seaborn · WHO/EPA Standards
